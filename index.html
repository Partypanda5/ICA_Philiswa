Ukuzala – Ukuzelula!

INTRODUCTION:

Ukuzala – Ukuzelula! is loosely translated as ‘ Through procreation, one is stretching one’s bones’ or ‘To give birth is to stretch myself’ or ‘To give birth is to increase my help or strength’. Through marriage, the importance of having children is believed to be what enlarge and extend one’s connection with humanity, one enlarges and releases one’s spirit, indeed, one’s being is enriched and enables
